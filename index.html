Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to order?

No — you can browse the menu, add items to your cart and place an order without signing up. Creating an account is optional and lets you track past orders and save favourites.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash on Delivery and UPI are both supported at checkout. Card and net banking support is coming soon.

Can I track my order?

Yes. After placing an order you'll get an order ID — enter it on the Track Order page any time to see its live status.

What's your return/refund policy?

Food quality issues are taken seriously — contact us on WhatsApp or call within 30 minutes of delivery with your order ID and we'll make it right with a replacement or refund.

Do you offer drive-through or dine-in?

Both are available at the Allagadda counter, along with no-contact delivery for online orders.
